The fragrance "Le Male" was created by the French fashion designer Jean-Paul Gaultier. It was launched in 1995 and quickly became iconic in the world of men's fragrances. The creation of "Le Male" was a collaboration between Jean-Paul Gaultier and perfumer Francis Kurkdjian. "Le Male" is known for its distinctive blue and white striped bottle shaped like a male torso, reflecting Gaultier's penchant for innovative and bold design. The scent itself is a masculine and oriental fragrance with notes of mint, lavender, vanilla, and amber. The success of "Le Male" can be attributed to its unique combination of fragrance and design, capturing attention and setting it apart in the competitive fragrance market. The collaboration between a renowned fashion designer like Gaultier and a skilled perfumer like Kurkdjian contributed to the creation of a fragrance that became a classic in the world of men's colognes.

Ultra Male
